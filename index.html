About Us















Utsaha, a proud techno-culture fest is a yearly, inter-college fest

organized by the students of BMSIT&M. At UTSAHA, we bring together

music, brands and college students to create unforgettable experiences.

Starting off as a bud in the year 2004, this fest has only grown and

become a larger endeavor with the passing of each year. It was initially

an interdepartmental fest but is now a platform where various colleges

from all over the state come together and take part in various events,

upholding the spirit of the fest. Also due to the popularity of Utsaha,

we have performers from all over the country whose performance is a

treat to watch. We also have shows like those by popular death/thrash

metal band Inner Sanctum and Counter Plot Theory which was organised

by an alumnus, as a part of Utsaha 2015.
